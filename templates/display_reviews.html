HOW ITS WROKS

The TINY TOT website is an innovative online platform mainly focused on kids. The website provides the tips for your kids to stay healthy. The website is designed to cultivate well being of students from LKG to 5th standard. It motivates your children to improve and maintain their health, prevent diseases, and reduce risky behaviors. This online platform provides animation videos, quizes, and minigames. The creative activities are divided to the users based on their ages.

home:When you click on home you entered to home page.

review:When you click on review you can add the reviews on the website and can submit the review.The people who is not registered ,they can see the reviews on the home page after clicking review.

extra activities:When you click on extra activities you will entered to the activity page that includes animation videos,quizes and minigames.If you choose animation videos you can see the videos.If you choose quiz you can attend the quiz.Otherwise if you choose minigames you can play the minigames based on their ages.

my account:When you click on my account in home page you can see your profile and you can update the profile.
